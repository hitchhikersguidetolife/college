Welcome to Staten Island Robotics!

Staten Island Robotics wishes to promote robotics throughout Staten Island by offering a program at which students can learn the basics of engineering, programming, and marketing. Many students who currently reside on Staten Island do not have access to intimate knowledge in these fields. It is our sincere hope that our students will continue to engage in the STEM fields and enter those professions. They will become the engineers and programmers of the future who will benefit Staten Island.
