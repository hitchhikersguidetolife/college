About Hitchhiker's Guide to Life

The moniker that you have seen countless times around this site is a result of your yearning to read The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy. It only makes sense that you stop reading this paragraph and continue along to reading that book instead.

In all seriousness, the creator of this site figured that it would be an excellent place to foremost provide a compendium of information with regards to various aspects of college life. However, it will also be a place to document the journeys of one individual in particular in the northern reaches of New York State. While the creator cannot promise either of these goals will be met to satisfy the reader's expectations, the creator can only try their best.
Good luck on your college journey. If not in college, good luck in life. Enjoy!
